Self-help can be a useful first step in dealing with your problems if you’re unsure whether to seek further help.

Change is difficult but even ingrained habits can be altered. It's never too late to change.

 Self help is ... Doing things yourself to try and solve problems. 'Group' self help is providing support and help for each other in an informal way. Setting and achieving goals ... Set small, achievable goals and review them regularly. this will build motivation and self-control Breaking habit formation ... Habit formation is the process by which new behaviors become automatic through repetition, it is possible to form new habits and break with old ones. Self help groups ... A self-help or mutual support group is any group which offers emotional support and practical help. see the link at the bottom to find a group near you.
