You are a carer if you provide (unpaid) support and care for someone who has an illness, disability, mental health problem or addiction. Being a carer can be mentally and physically tiring. You may also feel isolated or be struggling financially.

There is a growing carer support movement in the uk. You can get assistance with a range of carer issues, some of these are listed below.
