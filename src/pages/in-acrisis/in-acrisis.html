am i in a crisis?

a crisis can be very different from person to person. if you answer 'yes' to any of the following questions, you may be in a crisis.

  • do you feel as if your mental health is close to breaking point?
  • are you having thoughts of suicide or self-harm?
  • does your behaviour feel out of control?
  • are you unable to control your alcohol or substance intake?
  • are family/friends more concerned for your safety than you are?
  • are you experiencing sensations around you that arenâ€™t real (delusions, hallucinations, hearing voices)?

what should i do?

speak with someone now if you urgently need medical help or advice, call:

nhs emergency and urgent care service: 111

tyneside crisis number: 0303 123 1146

samaritans: 116 123

for urgent medical attention visit your local accident & emergency (a&e) or book an emergency gp appointment.
